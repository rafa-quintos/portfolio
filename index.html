About Me

Hello! I'm Rafa Quintos

My love affair with data began with an introductory Statistics course that left me begging for more. Soon, I was neck-deep in Business Analytics, mastering Excel, SQL, and anything else with a spreadsheet pulse. But even that wasn't enough. I craved the deeper stories hidden within, so I devoured Tableau, SPSS, and even wrangled Python and R like trusty data steeds. Fuelled by ramen and ambition, I conquered each dataset like a final boss, graduating ready to unleash my analytical prowess on the world.

Now, fueled by curiosity, I love solving puzzles with a sprinkle of analytical magic, whether it's optimizing frozen food pipelines (yes, cold storage adventures are a thing!) or leading clients to data-driven bliss. But the data dragon never sleeps, so I'm constantly sharpening my skills, always on the hunt for the next big insight.

My dream? To wield data like a force for good, unravel its mysteries, and make a positive impact across the board. So, if you have a business puzzle missing key pieces, contact me!
